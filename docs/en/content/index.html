Ai Ohto is the main character in the anime Wonder Egg Priority.

Character¶

Base status¶

Hunger 200 / health 150 / sanity 200 / attack 1x

Dream enhancement¶

Ai can regain more health and sanity in sleep, but the speed of food will consumption be faster.

Negative effects¶

I haven't decided yet. How could such a lovely girl have cons?

weapons¶

4 color ballpoint pen¶

It's a huge ballpoint pen. It takes a lot of effort to wield the ball point pen. And it really hurts!

  • Crafting: Quill x1, Glass Balls x1
  • Speed: -10%
  • Durability: 100
  • Damage: 250
  • Strike Duration: 0.66s + 0.42s
Hints
  • Ballpoint pen bursts with high damage, but its sustained damage is ordinary.
  • Unequipping weapon manually can skip post-strike animation.

Penlight¶

Can illuminate and hack monsters. The stronger the light, the higher the damage. Charge with firefly and bulb flower.

  • Crafting: lantern x1, electric horn x1
  • Lighting time: 500 seconds
  • Damage: 15 (extinguished), 45-75 (lit)

Golden Ribbon -- under development¶

Swing like a serpent, dealing damage to multiple targets over a wide area in front.

  • Crafting: whip x1, spider silk x5
  • Durability: 150
  • Damage: 40
  • Can be repaired with sewing bag and tape.
