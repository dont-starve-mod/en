Introduction

This mod has stopped updating.

If you want to take over, feel free to modify or extend this mod.

  • Steam workshop

Character¶

Ai Ohto is the main character in the anime Wonder Egg Priority.


Basic Properties¶

  • Hunger: 200
  • Sanity: 150
  • Health: 200

Dream Bonus¶

While sleeping, Ai regains more health and sanity, but also consumes more hunger.

Weapon¶

Ai Ohto can craft three exclusive weapons, which can be used by other players. She brought her ballpoint pen when she entered the Constant.

Ballpoint pen¶

A huge ballpoint pen. You have to swing it with some force. It's no joke if getting hit.

Penlight¶

The more intense the light, the higher the damage. Recharge with firefly and light bulb.

Golden ribbon¶

It can cause damage to multiple targets in a wide area in front of it.
